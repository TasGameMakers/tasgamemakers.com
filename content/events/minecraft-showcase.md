The **Minecraft Showcase** is a competition for Tasmanian school students!

**More information coming soon.**

## Key Details

- **Opens:** Friday 27 February 2026
- **Submissions close:** Friday 10 April 2026
- **Open to:** All Tasmanian school students
- **Submissions:** Individual or team entries welcome
- **Submissions must** come from a parent, guardian, or teacher on behalf of the student(s)

Selected submissions will have a chance to be featured at the Level Up Tasmania Expo in April!
