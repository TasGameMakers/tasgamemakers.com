Level Up Tasmania Expo

A two-day public expo showcasing Tasmanian game development talent.

← Back to events
Date: Friday, April 17 – Saturday, April 18, 2026
Time: 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM AEST
Location: PW1, 1 Hunter Street, Hobart
Open in Google Maps

Level Up Tasmania is a celebration of Tasmanian game development.

Friday 17 & Saturday 18 April at PW1 in Hobart.

Come and play games made by local creators, meet the developers, and experience the best of Tasmanian game development!

For more information, visit Screen Tasmania.

Supporting Tasmanian creatives. We're inclusive, creative, passionate about making games, and proudly Tasmanian.
