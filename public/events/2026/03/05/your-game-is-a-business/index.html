Punch up your business skills with this workshop presentation from games business development consultant Jon Cartwright.

Calling all Tasmanian game developers! Want to level up your business acumen?

(The answer is yes, yes you do).

In the lead-up to Level Up Tasmania 2026, Screen Tasmania is welcoming games business development consultant Jon Cartwright for an afternoon of bizdev upskilling for Tasmanian gamemakers.

This workshop will cover:

  • How to effectively manage risk
  • Publisher contracts and terms — what to look out for
  • How to define and deliver your production milestones
  • How to prepare for commercial delivery and beyond
  • and more!

This insightful afternoon session will include light refreshments.

Who can attend?

Presented by Screen Tasmania, this is an open event for anyone who fits the following criteria:

  • You must be a Tasmanian resident, aged 18 or over.
  • You must be actively working on a digital game.
  • Priority will be given to people exhibiting at Level Up Tasmania 2026.

Tickets are free but registration is essential to confirm numbers.
